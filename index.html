:- About The Company

Apollo Pools is able to deliver turnkey solution to all types swimming pools from its concept design to completion. We are now engaged in the import, supply, fabricati in and trading of materials, spares and equipments which are essential for designing and developing residential swimming pools, commercial swimming pools, competition or olympic pools, practice pools, diving pools, lap pools, water polo pools, kids pools, fountains, sauna, steam room and Jacuzzi installations.

The services that we provide includes concept design, remodeling, excavation, construction, swimming pool waterproofing, pool tilling, water treatment, wooden deck installation, swimming pool lighting, filtration and maintenance for all type of swimming pools at a budget that the customers can afford.

Apollo Pools offers a lot of unique swimming pool designs and types of swimming pools to its clients. The Swimming Pools can be constructed in any shape and size. It can be small or big, round or square or oval. We design and develop the swimming pool that the customers wish to become; by understanding their needs and budget. Our team of professionals includes architects, designers, engineers and workers who are well experienced in swimming pool industry..
